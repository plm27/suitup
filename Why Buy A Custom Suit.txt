Why Buy A Custom Suit? 
-save time from going to different stores to find the right suit
-you can’t find a suit that fits your body type, eg, you are very tall or short.  Maybe you have a large belly and want a more slimming look. 
-customization: add details and better fabric selection. 
-better quality: better investment
-you want to feel confident
-you have a unique taste that a normal store doesn’t cater to.
-it can give you a more professional demeanor and advance your career.
