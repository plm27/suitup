Our Process

We strive to understand our clients through our time-tested four-step process
  • Meeting
    • Book an appointment with one of our stylists in one of three easy ways: 1) submit your information online, 2) call us, or 3) email us. When you arrive at our showroom, our experts will ask you some prudent questions to figure out your desired outfit.
    Example: you want a a classic warm weather suit that you can wear to the office or a night out on the town.

  • Selection
    • Once you decide on your outfit, our stylists will guide your through our fine selection of fabrics using our carefully curated swatch book. Our fabrics come in different weights, textures, and colors. We will also help you select the finer details of the suits, such as the lapels, buttons, pockets, lining, front closure, and venting.

  • Measurement
    • Once you are done customizing your suit to your tastes, our styilsts will take your measurements using our state of the art body scanners. We will note your measurements on our proprietary figure charts and send them to our tailors.

  • Fit
    • Our expert tailors will take center stage in this final step. They will use their experience and expert craftsmanship to deftly construct your suit. This process usually takes about 6-8 weeks. We will notify you upon completion so that you can come into our showroom to see if the suit meets your standards. If not, we will adjust the suit as needed. We guarantee that your suit will fit perfectly.
